Privacy Policy

Draft — last updated 2026-07-08. Pending legal review.

This document is a draft and is subject to change pending legal review.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how MapleGamma (the "Service") handles information when you create an account and use the Service. The Service provides general market information and simulated (paper-trading) results for educational purposes only, and is not investment advice.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address and authentication credentials (a hashed password, and/or Google OAuth identifier, and/or passkey public-key credentials). We do not store plaintext passwords.
  • Consent records: your acceptance of these Terms, the not-investment-advice acknowledgement, and your Quebec-residency attestation.
  • Session & security data: session tokens, and limited technical signals (such as IP address and device information) used for sign-in, trial eligibility, and abuse prevention.
  • Billing data: subscription tier and status. Payment card details, when billing is enabled, are handled by our payment processor and are not stored by us.

3. How we use information

We use this information to operate the Service: to authenticate you, maintain your session, determine your entitlement/plan, prevent trial abuse and fraud, and to communicate service-related matters. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data may be delayed or inaccurate

Market data shown in the Service may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate and is provided for general information only. See the Terms of Use.

5. Service not available in Quebec

The Service is not directed at, or intended for use by, residents of the province of Quebec, and is not available to Quebec residents.

Avis : ce service ne s'adresse pas aux résidents du Québec et ne leur est pas destiné. L'information fournie est de nature générale et ne constitue pas un conseil en placement ni une recommandation.

6. Data retention & your choices

We retain account and consent records for as long as your account is active and as required for legal, security, and record-keeping purposes. You may request deletion of your account by contacting the site operator.

7. Past performance

Past performance — whether actual or simulated — is not indicative of, and does not guarantee, future results.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to the site operator.
